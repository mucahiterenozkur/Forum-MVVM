Project Description:
Create an iOS project, which has 5 screens each with UI of your choice. Apps will consume mock service from this url: https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com/
The screens are:
- SplashScreen: It will be the starting point of the app. Show a logo for 1 seconds and then open the next screen.
- UserScreen: It will list users from this link: https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com/users
- UserPostsScreen: When clicking on user from list, another screen will list posts (only title) from this link: https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com/posts
- PostDetailsScreen: On the post item, there will be “Show More” button, which will take us post details screen. It will the clicked post title and body.
- PostCommentsScreen: in PostDetailsScreen there will be “Read Comments” button, that will take us to screen that will list comments from this link: https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com/comments
Projeyi MVVM ile yapmanız beklenmekte. Kütüphane kullanımı serbest
